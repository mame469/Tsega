About Us

Welcome to Tsega Milk, Tsega Milk is a popular local dairy restaurant and cafe in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, renowned for its fresh dairy products and unique dishes. It operates its own dairy farm, ensuring a farm-to-table experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide healthy, affordable, and high-quality traditional dairy products—like fresh milk, yogurt, and soft cheese—directly to the community.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the premier farm-to-table destination in Ethiopia, celebrated for sustainable, natural dairy and traditional Ethiopian meat dishes.
